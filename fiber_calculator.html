Instructions

  • Start Fiber # = the first fiber number in your count.
  • Target Fiber # = the fiber you are splicing.
  • SMF shows the 12 fibers for the tube and highlights the target.
  • Ribbon shows Ribbon #, fiber in ribbon, and ribbon color name.
  • Flex adds cable size and calculates the rolled ribbon location.
  • Tube stripes: 145–288 = black stripe, 289–432 = red stripe.
