Recipe Book

Recipe Book respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the mobile application.

Information Collection

Recipe Book does not collect, store, or sell personal information.

Personal Data

The app does not require account registration and does not collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, or location data.

App Data

All recipes, categories, ingredients, notes, and saved content you create are stored locally on your device.

  • No recipe data is uploaded to external servers.
  • No cloud database is used.
  • No personal information is shared with third parties.

Camera & Image Access

Recipe Book may request access to your device camera or photo library solely for the purpose of adding recipe images. Images remain stored locally on your device.

Web Import Feature

If you choose to import a recipe from a website, the app sends the provided URL to a third-party recipe extraction service to retrieve recipe content. No personal information is transmitted. Only the recipe URL is processed.

Third-Party Services

Recipe Book does not use advertising networks or tracking analytics. If a recipe extraction service is used, it processes only the URL needed to retrieve recipe information.

Permissions

Permissions are requested only when necessary for core functionality (such as camera access for recipe images).

Data Security

Your recipe data remains under your control and stored locally on your device.

Children’s Privacy

Recipe Book does not knowingly collect information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the app’s functionality changes. Any updates will be reflected on this page.
