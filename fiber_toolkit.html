What this web app offers

Fiber Tool Kit is built as a central landing page for practical fiber work resources. Instead of scattering tools across random files, this homepage acts like the junction box. From here, users can jump into reference material, calculation tools, and future add-ons as the toolkit grows.

The goal is simple: fast access, clean layout, and room to expand without rebuilding the whole site every time a new tool gets added.

Use it for

  • Quick OTDR reminders in the field
  • Basic fiber math and estimates
  • Loss planning before or during a job
  • A central place for future fiber helper pages
