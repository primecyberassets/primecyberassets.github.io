Privacy Policy

CabinetKeeper

CabinetKeeper respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the mobile application.

Information Collection

CabinetKeeper does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal information.

Personal Data

The app does not request or collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, location data, contacts, or any other personally identifiable information.

App Data

All information you enter into CabinetKeeper — including inventory items, store names, quantities, categories, and grocery lists — is stored locally on your device only.

  • No data is uploaded to external servers.
  • No data is shared with third parties.
  • No data is stored in cloud databases.

Share Feature

If you choose to use the built-in Share List feature, the grocery list text will be shared through apps you select (such as messaging or email applications). CabinetKeeper does not control how those external apps handle shared content.

Permissions

CabinetKeeper does not request sensitive device permissions. The app operates fully using local storage on your device.

Third-Party Services

CabinetKeeper does not use third-party analytics services, advertising networks, tracking tools, or external SDKs.

Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, your information remains fully under your control on your device.

Children’s Privacy

CabinetKeeper does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated if the app’s functionality changes. Any updates will be reflected on this page.
