Planning Notes

  • Use this for estimating expected loss before testing.
  • Singlemode common rough values: about 0.35 dB/km at 1310 and 0.20 dB/km at 1550.
  • A common connector pair estimate is around 0.30 dB.
  • Fusion splices are often planned around 0.07 dB each.
  • Splitters can hit hard, so make sure their loss is included.
  • Adding a small margin helps keep the math from being too optimistic.
