Privacy Policy

Recipe Book respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the mobile application.

Information Collection

Recipe Book does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal information.

Personal Data

The app does not request or collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, location data, or any other personally identifiable information.

Camera Permission

Recipe Book requests access to your device camera solely to allow you to take photos of recipes for text recognition (OCR scanning).

  • Images are processed directly on your device.
  • Images are not uploaded to external servers.
  • Images are not shared with third parties.

Camera access is used strictly for recipe scanning functionality.

Photos and Media Access

The app may request access to your device’s photos so you can select existing images to scan for recipes.

  • Selected images are processed locally on your device.
  • No images are transmitted outside your device.
  • No images are stored on remote servers.

Data Storage

All recipes, scanned text, and information entered into the app are stored locally on your device. The app does not use cloud storage or external databases.

Third-Party Services

Recipe Book does not use third-party analytics, advertising services, tracking tools, or external SDKs.

Data Security

Since no personal data is collected or transmitted, your information remains fully under your control on your device.

Children’s Privacy

Recipe Book does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated if the app’s functionality changes. Any updates will be reflected on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact the developer through the app’s Google Play listing.
