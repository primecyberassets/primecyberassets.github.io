Privacy Policy

Fiber Counter respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how information is handled when you use the Fiber Counter mobile application.

Information Collection

Fiber Counter does not collect, store, share, or transmit any personal information.

Personal Data

The app does not request or collect names, email addresses, phone numbers, location data, or any other personally identifiable information.

Device Access

Fiber Counter operates entirely offline and does not access device storage, contacts, camera, microphone, or network services beyond what is required to function as a calculator.

Third-Party Services

Fiber Counter does not use third-party analytics, advertising services, or external SDKs.

Data Security

Since no user data is collected or stored, there is no risk of data exposure through the app.

Children’s Privacy

Fiber Counter is safe for all ages and does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13.

Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated if the app’s functionality changes. Any updates will be reflected on this page.

Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, you may contact the developer through the app’s Google Play listing.
